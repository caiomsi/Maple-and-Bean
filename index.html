Our Story

Coffee worth slowing down for

Maple & Bean started with a single drum roaster and a simple idea: great coffee should feel personal. We source beans in small lots, roast them a few pounds at a time, and brew them the way they taste best.

More than a coffee shop, we're a corner of the neighborhood — the kind of place where the barista knows your order and there's always a seat by the window. Pull up a chair and stay a while.

  • Small-batch roasted on-site, every week
  • Ethically sourced single-origin beans
  • Baked fresh daily by our own kitchen
